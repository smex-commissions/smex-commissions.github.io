GUIDELINES:

  1. I have the right to deny any commission request!

  2. Full Render prices are based on each individual request and may increase past the base price through negotiation.

  3. Please be specific when describing the scene you want!

  4. Make sure to send character/pose refs!

  5. A half payment is due prior to sketching, and payment in full is due afterwards. (Full upfront payment also available)

  6. Tips and requests involving my own OCs, while not necessary are very much appreciated :3

Thank you so much for being interested in commissioning me! I truly appreciate the support and I look forward to hearing your commission requests!
