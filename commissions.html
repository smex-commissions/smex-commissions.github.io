GUIDELINES:

  1. I have the right to deny any commission request!

  2. Prices are based on each individual request and are negotiable, but start at $30 for 1 character!

  3. Please be specific when describing the scene you want!

  4. Make sure to send character/pose refs!

  5. Payment is due up front, either in part or the whole commission cost!

  6. If not paid in full, only a sketch will be completed until all payment is received!

  7. Tips, and requests involving my own OCs, while not necessary are very much appreciated :3

Again, thank you so much for being interested in commissioning me! I truly appreciate the support and I look forward to hearing your commission requests!
